Category guide

Choose the question set that fits the room. They get more intimate as you move down the list.

Happy Hour
Lightest set. Good for warming up, newer friends, or groups that still need a gentle entry point.
On the Rocks
More personal friend questions, with enough bite to make the conversation interesting without going fully nuclear.
Last Call
Deeper, more revealing prompts for close friends and late-game conversations.
Extra Dirty
Most explicit set. NSFW/adult questions, so leave this off unless everyone actually wants that version of the game.

Rules and card types

Basic rule: answer honestly or pass by drinking. No alcohol is required; use any agreed penalty or just skip.

Straight Up
Ask one person. If they give a satisfying answer, they get the card as a point. If they pass, ask someone else.
Make It a Double
Ask one person, then ask another. Give the point to whichever answer you like best.
This Round’s On Me
Everyone gets a chance to answer or pass. The asker gives the point to their favorite answer.
Digital discard pile
When a card is drawn, this site immediately moves it to the discard pile, so it will not repeat until you reset.

For two players, the printed game treats the special card types as optional. The simplest version is to alternate drawing and asking each other.
