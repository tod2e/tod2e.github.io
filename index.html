How to play

Rules and card types

Answer honestly, or pass by taking the agreed penalty. Alcohol is optional.

1Select sets
2Draw a card
3Answer or pass
4No repeats
Straight Up
Ask one person. If they answer, they can keep the card as a point. If they pass, ask someone else.
Make It a Double
Ask one person, then ask another. Award the card to whichever answer you like better.
This Round’s On Me
Everyone gets a chance to answer or pass. The asker chooses their favorite answer.
Digital discard pile
Once drawn, a card is moved to the discard pile and cannot appear again until you reset.

Two-player shortcut: ignore the special formats and alternate drawing cards for each other.
