How to play

Rules and card types

Answer honestly, or pass by taking the agreed penalty. Alcohol is optional.

1Add players
2Draw on your turn
3Ask, skip, or pass
4Award the card
Straight Up
The current player asks one person. If they answer, award that person the card. If they pass, ask someone else.
Make It a Double
The current player asks two people. Award the card to whichever answer the asker likes better.
This Round’s On Me
Everyone gets a chance to answer or pass. The asker awards the card to their favorite answer.
Skip question
If the current player does not want to ask the drawn question, they can skip it. No point is awarded, the card goes to the discard pile, and the next player draws.
Discard pile
Once a card is drawn, it cannot be drawn again until the discard pile is reset.
